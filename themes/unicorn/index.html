Unicorn Theme

This theme is used for testing and as a playground.

Breakpoints

Standard breakpoints for shifting of menu and large-scale elements. We use two breakpoints at 768px and 1200px width:

  • small: <= 767px (mobile)
  • medium: between 768px and 1200px (tablet, laptop)
  • large: >= 1201px (desktop)

Space

  • none: 0
  • xxsmall: 3px
  • xsmall: 12px
  • small: 18px
  • medium: 24px
  • large: 34px
  • xlarge: 42px
  • xxlarge: 64px

Typography

Roboto for headings (font-weight: 800), Arial for body text.
